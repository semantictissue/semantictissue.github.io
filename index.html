About

Prashanth is a front-end engineer and designer with a desire to make digitial experiences better. He excels at rapid prototyping, front-end development of web interfaces and applications, and creating pixel-perfect designs for production.

He designs and develops with user-centered principles, while also keeping the business’s needs in mind. Originating from a background in biomedical engineering, it has always been Prashanth’s passion and desire to improve the health and wellness of people through advances in technology – and now as a developer and designer, through simply great design.

Prashanth is currently consulting as a Front-end Engineer for Charles Schwab in San Francisco, CA.

He enjoys his fair share of time with Sublime Text or Photoshop in full screen. When not burning his retinas with pixels, Prashanth can be found hunting the Bay Area for delicious food.
